## ğŸ•¹ï¸ How to Play

1. Press **ENTER** to start the game.
2. Type your choice: `rock`, `paper`, or `scissors`.
3. The computer will randomly choose its move.
4. The program announces the winner of the round.
5. Repeat for **3 rounds**.
6. After all rounds, the final scores are displayed.
7. Press **`q`** anytime to quit the game.
